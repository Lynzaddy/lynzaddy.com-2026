Common Questions

Questions people ask
before they reach out.

The ones we'd want answered before filling out a form. Saved you the trouble.

Do you work with clients outside the Midwest?
Yes — and we always have. Most of our work is done remotely regardless of where a client is located. We're based in Omaha, but our clients are spread across the country. Geography has never been a factor in how we work.
How do you price projects?
Most projects are fixed-price after a defined scope. We give you a number before work starts — not a range, not an estimate. Hourly engagements are available for consulting, audits, and retainer work. We'll recommend the structure that makes more sense for your situation.
Can I hire you for just a few hours?
For new project work, we have a minimum engagement size — typically around $2,500. For existing clients or consulting work like audits and code reviews, hourly or short-block engagements are absolutely available. If you're not sure which category your request falls into, just ask.
Do you work with agencies or just direct clients?
Both. We've served as the WordPress partner for marketing agencies that don't have in-house dev capacity, and we work directly with organizations of all sizes. If you're an agency looking for a reliable WordPress partner to send client work to, we're open to that conversation.
How long does a typical project take?
Discovery and Architecture together run one to three weeks. A standard marketing site build typically takes four to eight weeks in Build. E-commerce and more complex projects run longer. We give you a realistic timeline during scoping — not an optimistic one we'll have to revise later.
What if I'm not sure what I need?
That's exactly what the first conversation is for. Tell us what's not working, what you're trying to accomplish, and what you've already tried. We'll ask the questions that help both of us figure out what the right engagement looks like — or whether one makes sense at all. No pressure, no pitch.
