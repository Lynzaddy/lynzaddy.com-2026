The Problem

Their IT team had built a WordPress site no one on marketing could update. Any change required a ticket, a two-week queue, and inevitably came back wrong. The marketing director was effectively locked out of their own website.

The Solution

Rebuilt the CMS architecture with a field-structured content model using ACF and a custom Elementor theme. Marketing can now update every section of the site independently, while IT retains full control over the deployment pipeline and hosting environment.

The Problem

An aging WooCommerce store with no staging environment, four years of plugin debt, and checkout conversion rates that had dropped 30% after a botched "optimization" by a previous developer. The owner had lost confidence in their own platform.

The Solution

Full plugin audit and cleanup, a proper staging-to-production pipeline, and a rebuilt checkout flow with Stripe and a streamlined cart. Conversion rates recovered within 60 days. The client now has a staging site and has never had to worry about an update breaking production again.

Client Name Here

Industry · Year

The Problem

A growing regional healthcare organization needed a website compliant with accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA) and HIPAA-safe contact workflows, but their internal team had no one with the expertise to get there. Their existing agency quoted six months and a five-figure retainer.

The Solution

Delivered a fully WCAG 2.1 AA compliant WordPress site with encrypted form submissions, no PHI stored on the server, and a clean handoff to the internal team. Completed in eight weeks. Cost them a fraction of the agency quote — with better documentation and a training session included.
